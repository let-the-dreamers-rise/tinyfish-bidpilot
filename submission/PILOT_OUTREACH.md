# Pilot Outreach Pack

## Cold Outreach Message

Hi [Name], I built **BidPilot**, a TinyFish-powered submission agent for proposal teams working inside procurement portals.

It is focused on one workflow: logging into a live portal, resolving required fields, mapping evidence, saving a clean draft, and stopping before final submit for approval.

I am looking for 15 minutes with proposal managers or capture leads who deal with repetitive portal work. If this is relevant, I would love to show the product and learn how your team handles bid submission today.

## Questions For Customer Discovery

1. How many bids do you handle each month?
2. How much of your time is spent inside portals rather than writing?
3. Which portal tasks are the most repetitive or painful?
4. Do you already use AI for drafting responses?
5. What would make you trust an agent enough to save a draft for you?
6. What actions would always require human approval?
7. If a tool removed 50 to 70 percent of portal work, how would you evaluate it?

## What Counts As Proof Of Demand

- 5 interviews with proposal managers or capture leads
- 1 team willing to pilot on a real portal
- 1 written message saying this solves a real workflow they currently do by hand
- 1 intro from a proposal consultant or govcon operator
